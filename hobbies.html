MY PERSONAL PROJECT

Aside from learning how to code and become a software developer, this is what I have been working on and what I have enjoyed building in my spare time for the last 3 years. As I mentioned in the video, working on cars is great as long as its just a hobby for me. This is my 2008 Lexus IS250. I swapped the engine out for Toyota's 2JZ engine from the iconic 4th generation Toyota Supra.

Being a one-off car, many parts had to be custom fabricated in order to set the engine and gearbox in the car. Because of the power goals, the engine is not the standard 2JZ engine. It has been fully built by yours truly with some of the best parts on the market. It features a single 72mm turbocharger from Borg Warner and makes over 1000 horsepower at the rear wheels. The gearbox and drivetrain have also been extensively reworked in order to handle this amount of horsepower without any issues. The engine is controlled by an AEM Electronics Infinity ECU in conjunction with the factory Toyota electronics to keep all factory amenities in working order.
This was by no means a one man job. I am very thankful to have had some of the smartest people in the automotive industry help me with it along the way.
